Salmon recipe:
Marinate with pepper and salt
Grill salmon for 5-10 mins
add onions, soya ketchup and fry for about 1 minute
add some chilli powder, vinegar and finally some sugar on top to caramelise the salmon

*Ingredients:
1) Onions
2) Salmon
3) Salt
4) Pepper
5) Lemon or Vinegar
6) Olive Oil
7) Butter
8) Soy Sauce
9) Brown Sugar
